StratifiedTable.test_equal_odds(adjust=False)[source]¶

Test that all odds ratios are identical.

This is the ‘Breslow-Day’ testing procedure.

Parameters

Use the ‘Tarone’ adjustment to achieve the chi^2 asymptotic distribution.
